Scalloped Potatoes

Description

In this tofu broccoli stir-fry, crispy tofu, broccoli, and water chestnuts are cooked with kung pao sauce and peanuts - a quick dinner option for weeknights. Serve over rice or noodles.

Ingredients(WIP)

  • Vegetable oil
  • extra-firm tofu cut in cubes
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Seasoning: Salt & Pepper
  • Additional: Crumbled Bacon/Cubed Ham

Steps(WIP)

  1. peel and slice thinly about 2 pounds of potatoes, and set aside in a bowl of cold water to prevent browning and heat your oven to 350.
  2. Whisk equal parts butter and flour, melting the butter in a skillet
  3. Season well
  4. Layer Potatoes
  5. Pour sauce
  6. Add crumbled bacon or other items per preference
